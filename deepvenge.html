Crystal Tycoon — A world of crystals, trade and strategy

Crystal Tycoon is our upcoming Roblox experience where players build crystal empires from the ground up. Mine rare crystals, automate production lines, trade with other tycoons, and expand your island into an efficient, glittering empire. We’re designing deep progression, fair economy systems, and playful visuals so both casual players and hardcore tycoons can enjoy the climb.
